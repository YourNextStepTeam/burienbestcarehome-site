# Burien Best Care Home - Research & Content Library

This repository contains the research foundation, content strategy, and published content for burienbestcarehome.com.

## Research (`research/`)
ICP research and competitor analysis that inform all content and design decisions.

## Strategy (`strategy/`)
SEO strategy, copywriting framework (StoryBrand), and content philosophy documents.

## Blog (`blog/`)
15 long-form blog articles (April 2026) targeting high-intent search queries from adult children researching care options for aging parents. Topics include sundowning, caregiver burnout, financial planning, touring guides, and post-placement adjustment.

## Social (`social/`)
Daily social media content (April-May 2026) designed to build trust and drive traffic to blog content. Each post maps to the content strategy and ICP journey stages.
