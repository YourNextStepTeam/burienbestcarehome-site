What to Expect When You Visit

We understand that visiting a care home for the first time can feel overwhelming. Here is what you can expect when you come to see us, so there are no surprises.

1

A Warm Welcome

When you arrive, you will be greeted by a member of our team. We will offer you something to drink and take a moment to get to know you and your family's situation.

2

A Tour of Our Home

We will walk you through every room of our home, from the shared living spaces and dining area to private bedrooms, outdoor gardens, and activity areas. You will see where residents spend their days and what daily life looks like.

3

Meet the Team

You will have the opportunity to meet our caregivers and ask them questions directly. We encourage you to observe how our team interacts with current residents.

4

An Honest Conversation

We will sit down and discuss your loved one's needs, your concerns, and your questions. This is a no-pressure conversation. Our goal is to help you make the best decision for your family, whether that is with us or elsewhere.

5

Next Steps (Only If You Are Ready)

If you feel Burien Best Care Home might be the right fit, we will explain our intake process and answer any remaining questions. If you need more time, that is perfectly fine. We are here whenever you are ready.
