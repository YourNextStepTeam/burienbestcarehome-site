# Research

Foundation research for the Burien Best Care Home website and content strategy.

## Files

- **icp-research.md** - Ideal Customer Profile deep dive. Primary persona: adult daughter (38-55) navigating care decisions for a parent. Includes demographics, psychographics, pain points, and decision journey mapping.
- **competitor-analysis.md** - Analysis of competing adult family homes and care facilities in the Burien/South King County area. Covers positioning gaps and differentiation opportunities.
