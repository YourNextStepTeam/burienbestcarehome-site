Compassionate Care, Personalized for Every Resident

Every person who lives with us receives a care plan built around their unique needs, preferences, and life story.

At Burien Best Care Home, we believe quality care starts with truly knowing each person. Our small, six-resident home allows us to provide the kind of attentive, individualized support that larger facilities simply cannot match. Below, explore the care services we offer and how each one is tailored to honor the dignity and independence of every resident.

Memory Care

Specialized Support for Dementia and Alzheimer's

Caring for a loved one with memory loss requires patience, specialized training, and a deep understanding of how to create comfort in moments of confusion. At Burien Best Care Home, our memory care program is designed around the principles of dignity, routine, and gentle engagement.

Our caregivers are trained in dementia care techniques that emphasize redirection, validation, and person-centered communication. We maintain consistent daily routines that help residents feel secure, while incorporating meaningful activities like music therapy, sensory stimulation, and reminiscence exercises.

Consistent Daily Routines

Reduce anxiety and confusion with familiar, predictable schedules

Person-Centered Dementia Care

Trained caregivers using specialized dementia care approaches

Safe Home Environment

Thoughtfully designed spaces that support security and comfort

Engaging Activities

Music, art, sensory experiences, and gentle movement therapies

Family Communication

Regular updates and involvement in care progress and decisions

Medication Management

Coordination with physicians and careful health monitoring

Our intimate setting means your loved one is never lost in a crowd. With a maximum of six residents, our team knows each person's history, preferences, and comfort needs.

Daily Living Assistance

Support That Honors Independence and Dignity

Growing older sometimes means everyday tasks become more challenging. Our daily living assistance program provides the right level of support, helping residents maintain as much independence as possible while ensuring their safety and comfort.

Whether your loved one needs help with bathing, dressing, mobility, or medication reminders, our team approaches every interaction with warmth and respect. We follow each resident's preferences and pace, because maintaining dignity is just as important as maintaining health.

Bathing & Personal Hygiene

Respectful assistance with grooming and daily personal care

Dressing & Mobility Support

Help with dressing and safe movement throughout the day

Medication Management

Careful tracking and administration of medications as prescribed

Nutritious Home-Cooked Meals

Three daily meals and snacks tailored to dietary preferences and needs

Housekeeping & Laundry

Clean, comfortable living environment maintained with care

Medical Transportation

Coordination and support for appointments and medical visits

Every care plan is developed in partnership with residents and their families, and it evolves as needs change. Your loved one's comfort and happiness guide everything we do.

Respite Care

A Break for You, A Welcome Stay for Your Loved One

Family caregivers are extraordinary people, but even the most dedicated need time to rest and recharge. Our respite care program provides short-term stays for your loved one in a warm, professional environment while you take the time you need.

Whether you need a weekend away, time to recover from an illness, or simply a chance to step back and breathe, your family member will be cared for with the same attention and compassion we give every resident. Many families find that respite stays also serve as a gentle introduction to adult family home living.

Flexible Stay Lengths

From a few days to several weeks, we accommodate your timeline

Full Care Access

All services and daily activities available during the stay

Personalized Care Plans

Developed before arrival to ensure smooth, comfortable transition

Regular Family Updates

Stay connected with communication about your loved one's activities

Smooth Transitions

Support for both resident and family during arrival and departure

No Long-Term Commitment

Flexible arrangement without ongoing obligations or contracts

Taking care of yourself is not selfish. It is essential. Let us care for your loved one while you recharge.

Post-Hospital Recovery

A Safe Place to Heal and Regain Strength

The transition from hospital to home can be one of the most vulnerable times for a senior. Our post-hospital recovery program provides a supportive, monitored environment where your loved one can heal safely with professional oversight and personalized attention.

We coordinate closely with hospital discharge teams, physicians, and therapists to ensure a seamless transition. Our caregivers support medication schedules, assist with mobility and rehabilitation exercises, and monitor recovery progress around the clock.

Hospital Coordination

Seamless communication with discharge planners and physicians

24/7 Monitoring

Professional oversight during the critical recovery period

Medication Management

Precise tracking and health monitoring throughout recovery

Physical Therapy Support

Assistance with exercises and mobility rehabilitation activities

Healing-Focused Nutrition

Nutritious meals specifically designed to support recovery

Transition Planning

Gradual planning for return home or longer-term care options

Recovery takes time, patience, and the right environment. Our home provides all three, along with the compassion and expertise your family member deserves.

What Makes Our Care Different

Personalized Care Plans

Every resident's care plan is built from the ground up, based on their health needs, personal preferences, life history, and family input. Plans are reviewed and updated regularly to reflect changing needs and evolving goals.

Family Partnership

We believe families are essential partners in care. We maintain open communication, welcome visits anytime, and include families in care decisions and daily life. Your involvement strengthens the care your loved one receives.

Small Home, Big Heart
