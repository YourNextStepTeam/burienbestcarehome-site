# Strategy

Content, SEO, and copywriting strategy documents for Burien Best Care Home.

## Files

- **seo-strategy.md** - SEO ranking strategy including target keywords, content clusters, and local search optimization for South King County.
- **copywriting-framework.md** - StoryBrand-based copywriting framework. Family is the Hero, facility is the Guide. Includes messaging hierarchy and CTA strategy.
- **content-philosophy.md** - Content voice and philosophy guidelines. Wellness brand aesthetic, compassionate and trustworthy tone, never clinical or institutional.
